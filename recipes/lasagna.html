Lasagna

Discover the essence of homemade comfort with my classic lasagna recipe. Perfect for any occasion, this lasagna brings warmth and satisfaction to every bite.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
